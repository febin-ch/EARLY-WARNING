Emergency Kit Checklist

Ensure you have the following items in your emergency kit:

  • Non-perishable food items for at least 3 days
  • Water (1 gallon per person per day)
  • Battery-powered or hand-crank radio
  • Flashlight with extra batteries
  • First aid kit
  • Personal medications and prescription glasses
  • Important documents (ID, insurance, bank details)
  • Cash and coins
  • Multi-purpose tool
  • Local maps
  • Cell phone with charger
  • Blankets or sleeping bags
  • Clothing and personal hygiene items
