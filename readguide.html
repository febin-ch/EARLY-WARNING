First Aid Guide

Learn basic first aid techniques to help those in need:

  • CPR (Cardiopulmonary Resuscitation): Follow the correct steps for chest compressions and rescue breaths.
  • Burns: Cool the burn with running water, cover with a clean cloth, and seek medical help.
  • Bleeding: Apply direct pressure to the wound, elevate the injured area, and seek medical help if bleeding is severe.
  • Choking: Use the Heimlich maneuver to help dislodge the object from the airway.
  • Fractures: Immobilize the injured area, apply a cold pack, and seek medical attention.
